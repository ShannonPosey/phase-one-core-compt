My Favorite Band Linkin Park

Bio

Linkin Park is an American Rock band. Current members are vocalist/rhythm guitarist/keyboardist Mike Shinoda, lead guitarist Brad Delson, bassist Dave Farrell, DJ/turntablist Joe Hahn, and drummer Rob Bourdon. Former members Mark Wakefield and Chester Bennington (March 20, 1976 - July 20, 2017). Originally from Agoura Hills, California. Categorized as alternative rock. Their earlier music was a blend of heavy metal and hip hop.
